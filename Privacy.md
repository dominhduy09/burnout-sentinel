# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Burnout Sentinel** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our web application or any associated browser extensions (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Service is designed as a student wellness tool to estimate burnout risk, and we only collect data that is strictly necessary to calculate your stress workload indicators and recommendations.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Data You Provide Directly
* **Planner Inputs:** To calculate your burnout risk score, you input weekly workload metrics including:
  * Number of weekly tasks and priority tasks
  * Estimated task and clinical hours
  * Sleep hours and estimated free hours
  * Self-reported stress levels (on a 1–10 scale)
  * Number of scheduled exams
* **Account Information (Optional):** If you create an account, we collect basic credentials such as email and encrypted session cookies to keep you logged in.

### B. Automatically Collected Data
* **Log and Device Data:** Standard web analytics and server logs provided by our hosting platform (Vercel) may collect metadata such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and request timestamps to monitor app stability and security.
* **Cookies:** We use lightweight session cookies solely to authenticate your login session and maintain application state. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We process and use your data for the following purposes:
1. **Burnout Risk Calculation:** To evaluate your workload indicators and return customized planning recommendations.
2. **State Management:** To remember your active session and allow you to toggle preset weeks.
3. **Service Improvement:** To debug and troubleshoot application performance and system latency.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

* **No Data Selling:** We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third-party marketers or advertisers.
* **Service Providers:** Your planner inputs are processed securely via our hosting provider (**Vercel**) using serverless functions.
* **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not include third-party tracking scripts (such as Google Analytics or advertising trackers) on our platforms.

---

## 4. Data Security and Retention

* **Security:** We use standard industry practices, including Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) encryption, to protect data in transit between your browser and our servers.
* **Retention:** Planner inputs are processed statelessly in serverless functions and are not persistently stored unless you explicitly save a week configuration using an account. You can request account deletion at any time.

---

## 5. Chrome/Browser Extension Specific Policy

If you use a Burnout Sentinel browser extension:
* **Single Purpose:** The extension operates solely to help you monitor student workload indicators.
* **No Background Collection:** The extension does not inspect your browsing history, read page content on external websites, or track you across the web.
* **Permission Disclosures:** Any requested browser permissions (such as storage permissions to save your local planner inputs) are used strictly for local feature enablement.
