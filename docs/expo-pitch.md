# Expo Pitch Notes

## One-Sentence Pitch

Burnout Sentinel is an early warning system for student burnout that predicts overload risk and gives healthier weekly planning suggestions before stress becomes burnout.

## Problem

Students in demanding programs often have more than 20 tasks each week, including classes, labs, clinical shifts, study time, exams, jobs, and personal responsibilities. Traditional planners can track tasks, but they do not tell students when their schedule becomes unhealthy.

## Solution

This project creates a planner that combines scheduling, burnout-risk prediction, and personalized planning advice. Students can see not only what they need to do, but also whether their week is realistically balanced.

## Why It Matters

Burnout affects sleep, focus, emotional well-being, and academic performance. A tool that helps students identify overload early could support better time management and healthier habits.

## Technical Angle

- Machine learning predicts burnout risk from workload and wellness indicators.
- An optional LLM translates that risk into practical weekly suggestions.
- The prototype focuses on usability for real student schedules.

## Suggested Hook

What if a planner could warn students they were heading toward burnout before they felt it?

## Possible Demo Flow

1. Show a packed weekly schedule with more than 20 tasks.
2. Show the planner flagging that week as high risk.
3. Show the recommendation panel suggesting a more balanced plan.
4. End with the public-health impact: prevention instead of reaction.
